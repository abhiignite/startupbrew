How strong is your team determines how far you will go.

We follow a rigrous and structured method to hire a great candidate for you. No we don't just collect resumes and forward you the list of potential candidates but take of complete end to end hiring.

First Round: We start with a basic round to check candidate's analytical skills and knowledge of basic algorithms.

Second Round: This round covers advanced data structure and coding skills.

Third Round: This round is optional and the questions asked here are related to domain in which the startup is working. company work specific and we ask questions ranging from http spec, rest architecture, databases, scalability etc.

Fourth Round: In this round we ask the candidate to build a small app based on the domain in which the startup is working. This helps gague the candidate's ability to think and work on a given problem from scratch with all given resources and stipulated time.

Last Round: We schedule a small chat with the hirinig startup CEO and the candidate. This is mostly to see if the candidate is a good cultural fit for the company or not.
